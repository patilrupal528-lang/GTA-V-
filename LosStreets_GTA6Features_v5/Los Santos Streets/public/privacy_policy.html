Privacy Policy

App Name: Los Santos Streets
Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

Los Santos Streets does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All game data (saves, settings, preferences) is stored exclusively on your device using the browser's localStorage API.

2. Data Storage

The following data is saved locally on your device only:

  • Game progress (health, money, score, weapons)
  • Character customization settings
  • Game configuration preferences
  • Auto-save snapshots (managed by the built-in GameAI system)

None of this data is sent to any server or third party.

3. Internet Access

The app requests internet permission solely for loading the initial web content within the Android WebView. No user data is transmitted over the network. The GameAI system operates entirely offline.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is rated for users aged 13 and above (Teen rating — simulated violence). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Content Advisory

Los Santos Streets contains:

  • Simulated violence (shooting, police pursuit)
  • Fictional crime themes in a cartoon-style 3D environment

This content is entirely fictional and not directed at real-world actions.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Play Store listing page.
